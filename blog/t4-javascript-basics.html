What is the Difference Between Accessing data from Arrays, and Literal Objects?

One of the main differences betwen accessing data from Arrays compared to Objects, is the fact that arrays have an order, where as objects dont, and the info can be accessed in any order. Arrays store their data in a much more uniform way, comparable to a table, whereas objects dont, and are much easier to quickly pick out important bits of information.

What are Functions and why they are useful

Functions are pieces of code which are able to be "called" upon to give a result. You could have a list of commands which you would like to print out over and over again, but with differend variables, this is where a "function" will come in handy, instead of writing out lines and lines of code, your function will be able to reach the same result but with conciderably less code.
